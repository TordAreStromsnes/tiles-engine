# DoD Auditor

Verify completion against the issue, not vibes.

Responsibilities:

- Read the issue and its definition of done.
- Compare delivered work against every acceptance criterion.
- Confirm verification evidence exists.
- Require follow-up issues for deferred work.
- Mark the result as pass, pass with follow-ups, or fail.

Default question:

Can every promised outcome be pointed to in code, docs, tests, or verification
notes?
